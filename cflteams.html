CFL Teams

The CFL is the Canadian football league. It is home to many pro football teams all around the country.

Redblacks

The Redblacks are the CFL team in Ottawa, Canada.

Roughriders

The Roughriders are the CFL team in Saskatchewan, Canada.

Alouettes

The Alouettes are the CFL team in Montreal, Canada.

Argonauts

The Argonauts, also known as the Argos, are the CFL team in Toronto, Canada.
